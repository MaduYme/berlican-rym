Internal · Battery Mechanism
Dual Pack Switching
Two packs under the floor. Active pack drives the motor. Standby pack charges via solar and regen braking. BMS switches in 4ms at 20% threshold — zero downtime.

Core Innovation
Dual Battery Active Switch
Two independent packs run in sequence. When the active pack hits 20%, the BMS switches to the standby pack in under 4 milliseconds. The depleted pack immediately begins recharging via solar and regenerative braking.
